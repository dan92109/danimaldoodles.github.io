Privacy Policy — Mexico Gas Converter

Developer: DanimalDoodles  |  Contact: info@danimaldoodles.com  |  Effective date: June 1, 2026

Overview

Mexico Gas Converter is a utility app that converts gas prices between Mexican Pesos per liter (MXN/L) and US Dollars per gallon (USD/gal). This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

The app does not require account creation, login, or any personal information.

Data stored locally on your device

The app saves the following values to your device's local storage so your last-used settings are restored when you reopen the app:

KeyPurpose
mxnPerLiterLast entered MXN/L value
usdPerGallonLast entered USD/gal value
mxnPerUsdLast fetched or manually entered exchange rate
lastEditedWhich field you last typed in
rateFetchedAtTimestamp of the last successful rate fetch
ads_removedWhether you have purchased ad removal

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to DanimalDoodles or any third party.

Exchange rate requests

When you tap Get Current Rate, the app makes a network request to open.er-api.com to fetch the current USD/MXN exchange rate. This request is a standard HTTPS GET and does not include any personally identifiable information. Please review the ER-API privacy policy for information about how they handle API requests.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the app displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address / general location
  • App usage information

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time via your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Remove Ads purchase

Purchasing the Remove Ads in-app purchase ($1.99, one-time) permanently disables all banner advertisements. After purchase, AdMob is no longer initialized or shown in the app.

In-App Purchases

The Remove Ads purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. DanimalDoodles does not collect, store, or process any payment card or billing information. Purchase records are managed by Google Play.

The app stores only a boolean flag (ads_removed) in local storage to remember that you purchased ad removal. This flag is not synced or transmitted anywhere.

Data Sharing

DanimalDoodles does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party data flows in the app are:

  • ER-API — receives your device IP as part of the exchange rate HTTP request
  • Google AdMob — collects advertising data as described above (free version only)
  • Google Play — processes in-app purchase transactions

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. It is a general-purpose utility app with no features directed at children.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data the app stores is kept only on your device. To delete it, uninstall the app — this removes all locally stored values. DanimalDoodles retains no copies of your data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL and the effective date will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
